About

Titan is aiming to make a significant impact on the lives of their customers (includes business entities) by creating devices in the personal accessories space (wrist watches, bags, wallets, belts and eyewear) that would help them simplify a part of their daily activities/ daily industrial requirements. The task is to first identify the problems/ personal use cases/ business use cases and then find specific solutions that can be embedded in the personal accessories or any other products for any kind of an industry. The problem should address a large base and the solution needs to be scalable.

Theme

There is enormous applications for micro motors in the market. The Micro Motors market has registered high growth in recent times on account of growing demand from the Automotive, home appliance, and medical equipment industries among others. With recent technological developments, high quality micro motors are finding rapid integration in various industries, thus resulting in increasing demand in the micro motors market. With new markets such as electronic information and 3D printing also increasing adopting micro motors the market is expected to register high growth. One of the reasons for this high growth rate has been the features/ use cases these products have been able to offer their consumers. There is a need to establish many more use cases in order for the category to continue growing.

TITAN being the fifth largest watch making company in the world, has expertise in making micro motors. TITAN has

  • Uni / Bi Directional Motors
  • Integrated / Independent Motors
  • High Speed Motors

with

  • Power Optimization
  • Positional Accuracy
  • FULL METAL MOTOR (Reliability / Serviceability)

On the back of successful watch makers, Titan realize the potential of this space and now are seeking to make significant applications for micro motors in diversified areas. To take this next jump it is important we create a pool of use cases that will help us develop a relevant pipeline of new products. These products need to address an apparent or latent customer need (customer can also be a corporate or business entity). But the first step is to identify the needs.

We believe a Hackathon can help in identifying consumer needs and their subsequent solution. With a large number of people applying their minds on one objective we are to identify many applications for micro motors. Since we are going to be a specific group of people we are confident that each idea proposed will be unique and the teams would be able to make proto at the second stage with support from TITAN.
